About me
I am a web developer based in Madrid who enjoys learning and sharpening his skills, but has a very bad memory and needs a place to gather his findings.

My daily tools of the trade are Python, SQLAlchemy, PostgreSQL, JavaScript, JQuery, HTML, CSS, Git, TurboGears 2, MongoDB, Nginx... So you can expect posts regarding any and all of these technologies.

This blog will help me keep track of software projects I’m working on and things I’ve learned. It is not intended to show off any spectacular knowledge. It is just a place for me to keep small snippets of code which I use recurringly but never manage to remember.

Feel free to contact me via Twitter.
